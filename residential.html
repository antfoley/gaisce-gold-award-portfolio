Residential / Intercultural Project

This project focused on fostering intercultural understanding and teamwork through shared experiences in a residential setting.

Community Development Retreat

For my residential project, I participated in a week-long community development retreat in a rural village. The project focused on improving local infrastructure and fostering connections with the community.

Key Activities:

  • Collaborated with a team of 15 participants from diverse backgrounds
  • Helped renovate a community center and build a playground
  • Organized cultural exchange events with local residents
  • Documented the experience through photography and journaling

This project was a transformative experience, teaching me the value of teamwork, adaptability, and the impact of collective effort in creating positive change.
