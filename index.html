About the Gaisce Gold Award

The Gaisce Gold Award is Ireland's National Challenge Award, encouraging young people to dream big and realise their potential through personal development and community contribution.

Skill Challenge

Developing a practical skill over a sustained period to enhance personal abilities.

Recreation Challenge

Engaging in physical recreation to improve health, fitness and personal wellbeing.

Community Challenge

Making a meaningful contribution to the community through voluntary service.

Adventure Challenge

Undertaking an adventurous journey that challenges personal limits and builds resilience.
