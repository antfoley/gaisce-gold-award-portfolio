About Me and My Chief Scout Award

My name is Anthony Foley, and I grew up in Athy. Scouting has been part of my life for over 14 years, and it's shaped a lot of who I am today. I've gone from being a young Scout learning the basics to now being a Rover and a Scouter, helping with Beavers, Ventures, and fellow Rovers. Along the way I've built skills, confidence, and plenty of memories.

I hold a Scout Chief Scout Award (Never had an opportunity to sign up for my venture one 😢). For the Gold Award I am working with Mary Fricker, who is supporting me through the process.

I decided to go for the Gold Award because I wanted a real challenge and a goal that would push me further. For me, it's about bringing together everything I've gained through Scouting, adventure, service, and teamwork and finishing my Rover journey with something that reflects that.
