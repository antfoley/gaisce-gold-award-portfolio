First Flight Lesson

The Plan

So the original plan was just to get used to handling the plane mid flight. Just to give me an idea of flying. This was just an introductory lesson. Nothing too complex.

The Do

So Stephen, the instructor, took off, climb to an altitude of 2000 feet (said our sensors) and let me take over then. Weather was not the clearest so instead of flying classic VFR, I was given headings and told when to turn. Around Trim, up to Athboy and then back to Weston. Stephen took over and landed when we were just north of Kilkock.

The Review

Absolutely loved it. So actually applied for my PPL now. And now its also my Skill of my Rover Chief Award.

Date: October 3, 2025

Flight Centre Tour

The Plan

So this was not a lesson, but a tour of the flight centre. Got to see the briefing room, the simulator and the actual planes. Got to see a bit of how everything works.

The Do

So we started in the briefing room, where we got a bit of a talk about the school and how everything works. After we had a tour of the entire building, including the simulator room. Finally we went airside to see the planes. Given the weather was not great, we stayed in the hangers. Got to see a few different planes, including a Cessna 152 and a Tecnam P-Mentor.

The Review

Absolutely loved it. Got to see how everything works and got to see the planes up close. Weather could of been better though. Absolutely loved the P-Mentor. Really want to fly one of those.
