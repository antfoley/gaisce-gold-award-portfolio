Date: September 23th, 2025

Day 2: Beavers

The Plan

The plan was to teach recycling by using old jigsaws and cardboard boxes, painting them and turning them into an autumn-themed picture.

The Do

We gathered materials and worked together to repurpose them into a creative autumn display. The activity encouraged teamwork while showing how everyday items can be reused, The Picture is still not finished but the Beavers are excited to complete it in coming weeks.

The Review

The session went well, and the group enjoyed the painting process. Thankful it was poster paint because my hands and the group tables got very messy!

Date: September 29th, 2025

Day 3: Beavers

The Plan

The plan was to continue the autumn-themed picture using recycled materials, focusing on teamwork and creativity.

The Do

The Beavers worked together to add the paintings from last week to the cardboard base, creating a collaborative autumn display. The bark of the tree was made from scrunched-up painted brown paper and old cardboard.

The Review

The activity was a success. Us, the leaders, did run into a bit of a problem where the card base wasn't big enough to fit all the paintings. I ended up taking apart one of my old knotboards to use as another base. So we are continuing with two pictures instead of one.

Date: October 17th, 2025

Day 5: Ventures

The Plan

The plan was to explore new ventures and activities that the group could undertake together.

The Do

We brainstormed various ideas and settled on a few key projects that they could work on in the coming weeks. This included finishing their chief scout award for scout section and improving on badge work. We also talk about national events that they might be interested in and they seem eager to participate in Venture Sword. One of the new ventures wants to go skydiving and if you read the Skill section of this portfolio, its obvious I want to go too!

The Review

The session was productive. The new ventures are a little shy to be starting a new section. But I think with time they will settle in and enjoy the new challenges ahead.

Date: November 3rd, 2025

Day 7: Beavers

The Plan

The plan was to have a pioneering night with the Beavers, Teaching them various gadgets with bamboo and rope.

The Do

We started with a quick demonstration of how to make a simple gadget using bamboo and rope. The Beavers then got into small groups to try making their own gadgets. They made a variety of items, including a small tripod and a simple shelter with a jumper as a roof.

The Review

So they were a little louder than usual. Some other scouters didn't really like that. The beavers really enjoyed it though. I also forgot to get pictures so need to remember that next time!
