Date: September 23th, 2025

Day 2: Beavers

The Plan

The plan was to teach recycling by using old jigsaws and cardboard boxes, painting them and turning them into an autumn-themed picture.

The Do

We gathered materials and worked together to repurpose them into a creative autumn display. The activity encouraged teamwork while showing how everyday items can be reused, The Picture is still not finished but the Beavers are excited to complete it in coming weeks.

The Review

The session went well, and the group enjoyed the painting process. Thankful it was poster paint because my hands and the group tables got very messy!

Date: September 29th, 2025

The Plan

The Do

Date: January 27th, 2026

Day 16: Beavers

The Plan

So the plan was to finally finish air activities by making parachutes and do St. Brigids crosses as a craft activity.

The Do

So we made parachutes using plastic bags, string and little plastic toy soldiers. Beavers really enjoyed making them and testing them out by dropping them from a height. The Review

Date: February 9th, 2026

Day 18: Beavers

The Plan

The plan was to have night of a valentine's day themed craft and a bit of backwoods fire safety

The Do

SO given the weather and how dark it got really quick, I ditch the fire idea. Ran a fun demo of backwoods tools needed for an activity. The kit included flint and steel, handsaw and emergency blanket. They loved everything. They all loved the flint and steel and all gave it a go. For the handsaw, we didnt want to use wood but wanted to give a demo and opportunity to try it out, so we used a piece of paper folded a few times and they all had a go at cutting it with the handsaw.

The Review

This went really well. Beavers really enjoyed the backwoods demo and were very engaged with it. The cards looked great but wasnt in the room so i dont think it was as fun as the backwoods demo.
