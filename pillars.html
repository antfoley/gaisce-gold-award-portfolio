Recreation Challenge

Marathon Training Journey

My recreation challenge focused on marathon training and long-distance running. This journey transformed not only my physical fitness but also my mental resilience and discipline.

Training Highlights:

  • Progressive training plan from 5K to full marathon distance
  • Participated in 3 half-marathons as preparation races

The discipline and perseverance required for marathon training has influenced every aspect of my life, teaching me the value of consistent effort and long-term commitment.

Adventure Challenge

Wild Atlantic Way Solo Trek

My adventure challenge was a 5-day solo hiking expedition along Ireland's Wild Atlantic Way, covering 80km of diverse coastal terrain while camping independently.

Journey Challenges:

  • Navigate using map and compass without GPS dependency
  • Manage food, water, and shelter for 5 days independently
  • Adapt to changing weather conditions on the Atlantic coast
  • Document the journey through photography and journaling

This adventure pushed me far outside my comfort zone and taught me invaluable lessons about self-reliance, risk management, and finding inner strength in challenging situations.
