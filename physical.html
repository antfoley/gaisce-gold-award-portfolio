Date: December 4th, 2025

Day 1: BJJ

The Plan

So the plan was to go to my first Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu class in over 4 years and get back into the swing of things. I was a bit nervous as I haven't done any martial arts in a while but I was excited to learn and improve my skills.

The Do

The class started with a warm-up, which included pushups, jumping jacks, and stretching. After that we moved on to drilling techniques such as shrimping, butt scoots, rolls, and technical stand-ups. Take down and submissions were also practiced. Finished with a few sparring rounds to apply what we learned.

The Review

I found the class to be quite challenging but also very rewarding. I was able to pick up on the techniques quickly and felt a sense of accomplishment after the class.
